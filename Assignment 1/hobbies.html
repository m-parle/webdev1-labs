My Interests

Gym

I have been going to the gym for over a year now, and so is a big part of my life.

From getting some time to turn off on a busy day, to keeping healthy, it remains part of my daily routine that I cannot do without.

The person who got me into the gym was my cousin! He owns a gym, and with his help and encouragement he gave me the confidence to not only go by myself, but to also enjoy it ☺.

Bikes

Since I was little I have loved bikes. Whether it was looking at them in magazines, or watching them pass by on the road, I always imagined myself on one in the future.

At 16 I got my first bike, a black and red Benelli BN 125 on my A1 license. Since getting it, I have rode it almost everyday for over a year. I love it!

Despite their appearance, the biking community are actually the nicest people ever, ironic right.

If i had to pick, my favourite bike would definitley be a Ducati Panigale V4S.

Cars

As with bikes, as a child i collected car magazines, went to car meets with family, and took photos of cool cars at any chance I could get.

My favourite cars will always be classics. Picking one favourite car would be impossible, but I will always love Mazda MX5's.

I recently got my first car, a Ford Fiesta Zetec sport. I love it and it is perfect for me! Since then, I have gone to just one car meet, where my car was a crowd pleaser. This will be the first of many attended.

Music

A common interest of many, I aswell love music.

I am the type of person to listen to any genre of music as long as i like the song. I do love older music, like 80's, however i also love newer music, like rap. I am not a big fan of pop music.

One of my favourite artists is Morad, a spanish rapper. I also love Fleetwood Mac, Oasis, and Drake.

Baking

I have loved to bake for as long as I can remember.

Whether it was brownies, a cake, or macarons, they will always be eaten in my house.

I love to try out new recipes and discover new foods. Despite it maybe not always turning out the best looking, they will always taste good

I find baking therapeutic however, anyone in the kitchen at the same time as me may disagree.
