✈ My Travel Adventures

My Recent Destinations

Tenerife

My most recent, and most visited destination. Tenerife remains a classic.

In my most recent visit, I stayed in the capital for the first time. It was beautiful with warm sunny skies, amazing culture, and tasty food to be found everywhere.

After experiencing both the North and South of the island, I must admit that the North wins the competition for me.

Gdansk, Poland

Despite having only visited Poland once, the mysterious city of Gdansk was my favourite destination of all time.

With my visit taking place at Halloween time, this city had the perfect mixture of eerie and cosy, with an amazingly calm atmoshpere all around.

This is the place where i feel I belong most. I will definitely be back!

Budapest, Hungary

My short stay in Budapest at Christmas time was spectacular and unexpected.

Although not the most popular city-break destination, Budapest was perfect for a short stay full of exploratin.

The winter temperatures however, results in you resembling a mixture between a snowman and a marshmallwo wrapped up in endless layers.

Lisbon, Portugal

Another city break that I crossed off my list - Lisbon.

Despite the weather being wet and rainy the entiritey of my visit, that didn't put a stop on my days of wandering.

I think Lisbon is quite an electric city, but also contains quiet, hidden gems down the small yet beautiful side streets.

Hopefully another visit is in the future for me (with better weather too).

Prague, Czech Repupblic

My summer visit to Prague is one containing lots of memories. My long awaited visit to see my friend after years since covid.

A city with lots of walking, but definitiely worth it for the breath-taking views of the city from above.

As lovely as the city was, the company was what truly made it so enjoyable.

Almeria, Spain

My not-so-recent, recent visit to Almeria in 2022 as part of an exchange was full of fun and laughter.

I met new people, stayed with a kind local family, and discovered areas all over the south of Spain.

Although Almeria is just a small sea-side town, I hope to return again in the future (maybe an old friend will even take me in for the week if I'm lucky).
